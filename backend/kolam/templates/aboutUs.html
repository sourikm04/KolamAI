Our Motive

Team CodeItUp from Meghnad Saha Institute of Technology proudly presents KolamAI for SIH'2025. Our project merges heritage with innovation, creating a digital bridge to preserve the timeless art of Kolam. By leveraging AI, we aim to archive these intricate patterns, foster a deeper appreciation for this Southern Indian tradition, and inspire a new wave of creativity for generations to come.
